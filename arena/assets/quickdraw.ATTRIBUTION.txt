Quick, Draw! Dataset
====================
The doodle tiles in the arena's "doodle-select" gate are a small sample (144 drawings across
animal / food / vehicle categories) from the Google Quick, Draw! dataset.

Source:  https://github.com/googlecreativelab/quickdraw-dataset
License:  Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)
          https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/
Credit:   "Quick, Draw! data made available by Google, Inc. under the
           Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license."

Only the stroke vectors (the "drawing" field) are vendored, as a de-identified sample, in
quickdraw.ndjson. No author/metadata is retained.
